Senior iOS Developer | Head of Mobile at Mindvalley

I’m crazy about adventures, all kinds of it, for the pleasure of being challenged and completing goals. I love to travel, getting to know new places, cultures, languages and people. I also love technology and how it makes our lives easier. I’m a curious person, always eager to learn new things and improve the way I work, my ideas, concepts and methodologies.
I like to adventure myself in different fields, and because of that, I’ve taken all sorts of opportunities. I have taught English for over 4 years, iOS Development for about 2 years, and I’m currently a Senior iOS developer and Mobile Lead in Mindvalley, a company located in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.
When people ask me what I do, I reply: I’m a wizard. A wizard? Yes, an iOS Wizard!Here’s why: 
Many people can develop iOS apps, but just a few can actually provide amazing and dazzling experiences to their users. 
I believe an app must do not only what it promises to do, but be much more than that. It must shine and be amazing, be something that users will use not only because they want to check the information it is providing, but also because they like playing with it, going through its features, discovering all it can do.
A great app must do great things, but yet, be simple. So simple that no tutorial is required, since the users just know where to go. It must be pixel-perfect, when a pixel to the right is not merely a pixel to the right. It must have an organised code with design patterns well-applied. It must use the coolest and most amazing techniques there are. It must have a do-it-all button, that when pressed, magic will happen. 
Building iOS apps, therefore, is not only about developing, but actually doing magic, and this is why I’m an iOS Wizard.
